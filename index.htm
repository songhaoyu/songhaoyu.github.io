About

I am Hao-Yu Song (宋皓宇), a student on conversational robot at the research center of social computing and information retrieval , and supervised by Professor Ting Liu and Doctor Wei-Nan Zhang .

I have received my bachelor’s degree from the school of computer science and technology in Harbin Institute of Technology at June 2017. And I am going to finish my master’s degree here in next 2 years.

Until now, my primary research interests mainly focus on dialogue systems, which is a challenging task in Natural Language Processing. Deep learning and reinforcement learning are always needed in this area.
